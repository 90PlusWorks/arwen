Artist Info:

ARWEN is an imaginary multi-medium artist based in the universe.

After graduating from the Western Desert Acadamy of Art, she began her freelancing career by creating commercial art in wood carving of sarcophagi for the royal family and their printing industry. Arwen has been creating commissions for thousands of years and were in the employ of Pharaoh Ramesses II from 1230 to 1213 BC. Some work consulted on or helped with during the ages are shown here. She currently does artwork and consulting for popular businesses such as Random Glow and Them drop-dead Apples.

Arwen: "I also love creating custom artwork for ordinary people such as Juan, Ren, Aam, John and Jane. I enjoy working with others to create a social experience and interconnected artworks.

In my free time, I like to swing on silk curtains while binge-watching my favorite TV shows.

Please email Arwen@protonmail.com to share your commission requests and learn more about my art process."
